About Our Platform

There are children in need of people who can donate books and money. These children are living in poverty, their parents cannot afford to buy them new books or pay for their school supplies and many do not have parents too. By donating books and money you can help these children get the supplies they need to succeed in school and please help them to make their tomorrow better.

It's not how much we give but how much love we put into giving.



Your small action of goodness can make someone's life better through
this platform you can donate books for the children in
schools , slums and shelter homes.

Donations are used to help children in following ways:

  • To provide them basic necessities like books, stationery and shelter.
  • To help them to get access to education and health care.
  • To provide them with livelihood opportunities so that they can earn a dignified living.
